help_outline Why use EComposer together with Ocolus?

Basically, please understand that EComposer and Ocolus are not related, which means you cannot configure themes using EComposer. By using EComposer you can create sections/pages as you want and use them in conjunction with Ocolus, for example you can create a section called testimonials to your liking and use them in conjunction with Ocolus's theme sections. Or you can build a landing page for your black friday campaign and use it regardless of whether Ocolus can do it or not. EComposer is a powerful page builder and if used with Ocolus or Kalles, it will give you a professional and eye-catching online store.
